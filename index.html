About Us

*SYNCTEX CO*, a global textile sourcing company. We specialize in sourcing high-quality textile products from South Asian vendors for markets across Europe and the USA, catering to the Home, Hospitality, and Institutional sectors, Apparels, and Active/Workwear. At SYNCTEX, we work closely with vendors who operate modern, state-of-the-art machinery and meet stringent quality, social, and environmental compliances, sustainable/Eco Friendly, green and conservational textile standards. Our highly experienced Quality Assurance team provides continuous support to production staff, ensuring that all goods meet our clients’ exact specifications.
